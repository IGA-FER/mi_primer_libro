# Author Presentation

This book has been prepared by Fernando Espejo Almodóvar.

## Short Bio

Fernando Espejo Almodóvar is a Tenured University Professor at the University of Salamanca (USAL), affiliated with the Higher Polytechnic School of Ávila. He is an expert in civil engineering and water resources management.

Among his academic responsibilities, he serves as deputy director of the Master's Degree in Water Systems Modeling at USAL. His research focuses on the integral water cycle, risk management in irrigation infrastructures, hydrology and computational hydraulics, as well as the application of artificial intelligence models in dam engineering.
